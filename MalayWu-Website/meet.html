Get to Know Malay Wu

An agent who truly cares about you and your family

My name is Malay Wu and I want you to trust me as your agent. As a mother to two young boys, I understand the challenges of family life and I appreciate that your time is valuable.

I take enormous pride in representing my clients with competence, professionalism and care. The most rewarding part of my job is when I get to take part in my client's first home purchase, upgrade to a larger space or relocating them to a new location. I value that trust bestowed upon me. Whatever your real estate needs may be I am willing to go that extra mile to help you and your family to make this transition as smooth as possible. It can be frustrating if you're not working with the right professional. With my personal experience of relocation several times both locally and internationally, numerous real estate transactions, and up-to-date knowledge of today's market and technology, I am equipped and qualified to work with you. Purchasing or selling your home is a major endeavor, let me help you navigate through this journey and together we will find success. Please give me the opportunity to learn more about you and your family.
